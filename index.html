About Freewill

| Advanced Center for Mental Health and Rehabilitation

At FREEWILL Advanced Center, we believe in the power of compassion and expertise to create positive change in people's lives. Our specialized team of clinical psychologists, pediatric physiotherapists, psychiatrists, and other professionals work together to offer exceptional care for emotional and psychological issues in children. We also provide counseling for learning difficulties and address developmental disorders like autism and mental challenges with utmost dedication and understanding. With a focus on personalized treatment plans and comprehensive assessments, we strive to improve the quality of life and overall well-being of our clients. Our commitment to accessibility and affordability ensures that no one is left behind on their journey to healing and empowerment. Join us at FREEWILL Advanced Center for Mental Health and Rehabilitation, where we are dedicated to making a difference and supporting individuals of all ages on their path to a brighter future.

Our Programmes

Play School

Welcome to our Playschool program, a nurturing haven designed exclusively for children with special needs. At our center, we recognize and celebrate the uniqueness of every child, fostering an inclusive environment where they can flourish. Our dedicated team of experienced professionals, including teachers and therapists, is adept at addressing various developmental, behavioral, and physical challenges.

With a personalized curriculum tailored to each child's requirements, we employ evidence-based methodologies to facilitate learning and growth. Beyond academics, our program emphasizes socialization, play, and exploration—essential components for young children's holistic development. If you have a child with special needs, we invite you to explore our Playschool, schedule a tour, and embark on a transformative journey with us. Together, we will provide unwavering support for your child's development and success, empowering them to reach their fullest potential.

Happy School

Welcome to "Happy School," our transformative Mental Health and Rehabilitation Program tailored for school students in Tirur Municipality. With a rising prevalence of stress, anxiety, and depression among students, exacerbated by the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic, our program aims to provide comprehensive support to address these pressing mental health issues.

Freewill Advanced Centre for Mental Health and Rehabilitation is committed to empowering the next generation to thrive. Through evidence-based interventions and personalized care, we envision improved academic performance and a healthier school environment. Join us on this journey of fostering resilience and well-being, making a positive impact on the lives of our future leaders.
